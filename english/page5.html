Mankidevi died young. Ghatal Devi was married to Vijedyadidutta Kardiv Grave of Goa. Chandradeva's son Harpaldev alias Karandev was married to Gokalayati, daughter of Shilahar Raja Bharati. He lived in Sangameshwar and Bhudargarh. Chandradev's son Vaitugidev was also called Govinddev. He was married to Mangaladevi, daughter of Singdev Sindya. He used to live in Chandwad and Sangameshwar. Vatungideva had three sons Somdev, Mahadev and Patladeva. The first of Somdev's two wives, Padmavati, was the daughter of Shilahar king Gopaldev. Another Mankidevi is the only daughter of Palavi of Sangameshwar and she got the inheritance of her father's entire wealth of Vatan. Somdev used to live in Someshwar. He was a scholar, charitable and mighty. He was a devotee of Mahalakshmi from Kolhapur. Nahadev used to live in Badami. Patladev used to live here at Dharwad Havana. These two brothers were telegraphed along with Mulanbal, the orators of the National Revolution in Konkan Karnataka.

Rudradeva was the son of Somdevdha, whose wife Sriyadevi was the daughter of Chandraditya Maurya. He lived in Bankapur and Sangameshwar. Daughter of Yadav by Latadevi, wife of Rudradeva's son Bankideva. His second wife Chandradevi was the daughter of Tijaydev Sindhiva of Torgal. Lived in Habankapur and Tapi and Ajanagiri. He had two sons Chandradev and Bhimdev. Chandradev was married to Mauladevi, daughter of Ishwardev Singhiya. He used to live in Ajanagiri. Telbadeva III came to live with his father after his defeat at Kalyani. Bhindev was married to Kongaldevi, daughter of Shilahar Kesiraj of Thane. He came into service in Sakae 1084 against Prataparudra on the side of Teldeva. Chandradev's daughter Deval Mahadevi and son Kamdev Deval Mahadevi were married to Vir Someshwar Ballal. The first wife of Kamadeva was Padmaldevi, the daughter of Shilahar king Gadharapitya of Kolhapur. Duttari Manika Devi, the daughter of King Galraditya, was given to her by the Shilahat king some villages around Babave and Bhushargad. He was the fourth Telbadeva TARY with the title of Kalyaneshwaradhipati.

This was Mahamandaleshwar. At this time some villages in Durgavati district near Kalyani were captured by Mahamv Tagal. He also accepted the right to recover and maintain welfare. Kamdev had two sons Someshwar and Virabhadreshwar and a daughter Deval Mahadevi also called Panchaldevi, Someshwar was married to Sriyadevi. She is the daughter of Sidhiya of Torangan. Someshwar was a mighty strategist. He was Mahamandaleshwar of Konkan Karnataka. He fought against Mahadev Yadav of Devgiri with the help of the king of Konkan. As Yadav's army was vast, he was defeated. He drowned in the sea while going to Gomya, Sirbhadreshwar's wife was Rudravashi, the daughter of the Telanganadha king Ganapati. Ganapati Raja and two daughters had no son. So he insisted on becoming Dirabhadra Ishwar and his heir. But he rejected the promise of his ancestors. From this it can be seen how the men of this family behaved according to their ancient traditions. Rudrava assumed the name Rudradeva and ruled Telangana. She was mighty and full of statesmanship. On the advice of Vibhadreshwar, Sarangpani, king of Devgiri, was sent to Telangana.

Due to this, Devgiri's Yadav broke up with him. Someshwar had three sons Kamdev, Karnadev and Banghadeva, Kamdev was married to Kamaladevi Hija, daughter of Vibhavanmal Kadamba of Goa. As there brother Sashtadev had no children, Kamadeva became the ruler of Goa. Karnadev was married to Parvati Devi, the daughter of Shilahar Someshwarva of Thane. He lives in Sangameshwar. The homelands of this family in Bankapur Honawar and Dharwad were also under his supervision. Bandhdev was married to Virdev Scindia's daughter Vijayadevi. He lived with his cousin Virabhadraswar. This answer is also found to have gone to Nohimi in the Hindavi kingdom. But finally started living in Bhupargad. When Malik Kapur attacked Goa, he had an epic fight with Karnadev. Wounded in battle. Navale near Bhughargarh died in 1239 CE at Patgaon. Here is his umbrella. Kamdev had three sons Harpal Dev, Bhimdev and Somdev alias Govind Dev, Harpaldev was married to Tila Devi, daughter of Kamdev Yadav of Devgiri. Daughter Ekveer Mati was kept close by the King of Devagiri. Shankar Yadav, son of Ramdev Yadav of Devagiri, stopped paying tribute to the Sultans of Delhi and was killed in 1234 CE.
