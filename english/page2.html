Ketyanavarma continued the fight for many years with the help of his brothers the Raja of Bijapur. Ketiva's son Telpadeva, his son Vikramaditya's son Bhimadeva and his son Adhyanadeva were the queens of Rashtrakuntha king Krishnadatiri. That increases its importance. His son Vikramaditya regained some of his ancestral kingdom. The name of Sya's queen was Bontadevi and she was the daughter of King Lakshmana of the Four Lands. His son Telpadeva Sya's queen Lakshmi Devi was called Jalal Devi. She is the daughter of a Rashtrakuta king named Karka. He regained the kingdom of his ancestors, re-established the Chalukya dynasty after two hundred years and assumed the title of Vikramaditya and announced the establishment of his independent kingdom in 896 Srimukhnam Samvatsare. By stratagem and prowess he regained his ancestral kingdom of Lata and Gujarat and began to rule there independently. His nephew Awazi Verma's daughter Nehaldevi Highe was married to Hopya Rajatha Surraja. So he gave them good help to get back the state.

Telbhadev has two sons Satyasriya and Queen Ambika Devi of Dasvarma Satyasriya, Queen Bhagaldevi of Dasasharma has two sons and one daughter. Son Vikramaditya Ayandegi and daughter Akkadevi Titha Savati's son Jay Singh, after attaining the title of Ratyasreya, Vikramaditya became the ruler of the kingdom. After him Ayandeva came to the throne. His queen Chandrika Devi was the daughter of Rashtrakuta Krishnadev. With that brother Jai Singh, he created a quarrel over the division of the kingdom. Even after Ayandeva renounced Ripurna Rajya, he continued to go to Tar Devli towards Kuswais. His sister Akkadevi was married to King Shastadeva of Kapalamba dynasty of Goa. This great warrior was intelligent and brave. To feed Apanadeva's family, he took over some parts of Konkan Karnataka and started independent rule from Vikramapur. Ahman Dev stayed near her for some time. He was fond of studying astrology and divination. He wrote two books, Vir Hamira and Ayan Nasha Ate. Hedonhi Granth Ayandeva. It was written by Panatu Bolungi Deva alias Govind Deva from the Shastri Pandits of Varanasi. This is evident from old documents.

Ayandeva established Kalyani from Yetagiri as the capital. Later its glory was increased by his nephew Someshwar. Aja Telpadeva of Adyandev is considered to be the first man of the Kalyani branch. Because he moved Chandi Ghol Hangal, Belgaon, Khutan and Mankhed, the capital of the Rashtrakuta kingdom, to Rathod Raja Ghanakam and made it a vast kingdom. Parmar king of Malwa was killed in a battle. He stayed at Mankhed for a few days and ruled. Further, the capital was taken from Mankhed to Yetagiri fifteen koras. Later Agyyandev took Kalyani and Rajdhani. Akkadevi, Sakhki younger sister of Adhyaan Deva, took care of his children. Jai Singh's mother's name was Bhagyavati Jai Singh Ha Goth: The mighty defeated the Balish Chola king Rajendra and four kings. Dethroning the Parmar Raja of Malwa, Bhon Raja recovered the kingdom of Gharangiri from his brother. When his mandaliks Kadibh and Yadav wanted to be independent, they forced him to accept their mandalik status. He was succeeded by his son Someshwar. Someshwar has held the titles of Aya Ballav Triloki Malla. Vyane defeated the Chola kingdom. Barkabij made peace with the king of Bhoj.

By doing, the previous enmity was removed. Pura Aamal was established on Mandalik Raja. A great warrior and a brave warrior, he acquired wealth by successfully conquering kingdoms like Kachi, Gor Bengal, Kamrukh etc. Kalyan increased the glory of the capital. His mother's name was Mohandevi, a Pallava princess, from Someshwar the gotra of this family became Bharvda. Because his royal priest belonged to Bhakandaji gotra. His two brothers Vikramaditya and Jaisingh Brikmaditya were brave, mighty and intelligent. Because of this, Someshwar was displeased with him. This led to fraternal fights. Vikramaditya imprisoned Samesvara and ascended the throne himself in 997 CE and became the sovereign emperor. He defeated the kingdom of Kadamba in Konkan and married his daughter Melel Devi. The investigation was carried out. He married a Chola princess, the Karat princess Rupmati Chandranmaladevi in Swayamvara. He defeated the Chavid Pandey Konkan, Hoysala and Chaula Rajas in several campaigns and captured the Chalkot region. Helped Narvanadiv, King of Malwa. He also carried out successful campaigns in Central Bengal and Assam and many states of North India like Gujarat.
